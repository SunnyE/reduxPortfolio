Let me tell you A little about myself

I am a diversely educated innovator. Coming from a background in finance, a minor in digital design and a Fullstack development bootcamp from Rutgers. Having a love of stock trading and not wanting the sum of my life’s work to be an increase or decrease in the bank account of somebody I barley know I’ve sought out for bigger things. To use my mind to create and design. To bring new products and experiences to people.
